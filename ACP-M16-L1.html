PizzaExpress is an international restaurant chain founded in London in 1965 by Peter Boizot, specializing in authentic hand-tossed, thin-crust Italian pizza. Famous for its dough balls and open-kitchen style, it operates over 360 locations in the UK and Ireland, with a strong presence in India, the UAE, and beyond.
